PLAYER GUIDES

Start stronger on Runeveil.

A clean overview of how new players can begin progressing on the server.

FIRST STEPS

Your first goal

Your first goal on Runeveil is to start earning currency. The best way to do that as a new player is by taking part in server events.

WHY EVENTS

Why events matter

Events reward players with currency and progression materials. They are one of the fastest ways to begin building real power on the server.

01

Join and Survive

Gather resources, move into the world, and start preparing yourself for future conflict.

02

Watch for Events

Stay alert for event announcements. Events are one of the best entry points into Runeveil progression.

03

Earn Currency

Currency fuels upgrades, repairs, and long-term progression systems across the server.

04

Upgrade Gear

Once you have currency, start improving your gear so you can survive longer and compete better.

05

Build Your Playstyle

Progression becomes deeper over time as players combine stronger gear with different ability choices.

06

Fight for More

As rewards get better, so do the players competing for them. Every step upward creates bigger stakes.

ARMOR ABILITIES

How abilities work

Some armor can gain passive abilities that activate while worn. These abilities can improve survival, defense, combat pressure, or utility depending on the build a player is trying to create.

PROGRESSION

What makes Runeveil different

Runeveil is built around staying invested in your gear and your build. Instead of ending at vanilla progression, players can continue pushing forward through upgrades, events, and better decision making.

NEED HELP?

Join the Runeveil community

If you have questions, want updates, or want to stay informed about server events and announcements, join the Discord.
