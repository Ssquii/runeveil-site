EVENT GUIDE

Why events matter

Events are one of the core gameplay systems on Runeveil. They reward players with currency, materials, and progression opportunities that can help launch a new player forward.

Because event rewards are valuable, other players will often compete for them too. That means events are not just about showing up. They are also about timing, risk, and survival.

PROGRESSION

How players grow stronger

Currency

Currency is used to fuel important progression systems. Earning it consistently is the foundation of growing stronger.

Upgrades

Gear can be improved beyond vanilla survival, making long-term investment in your equipment worthwhile.

Armor Abilities

Some armor can gain passive abilities that activate while worn, allowing players to create different builds and survival strategies.
