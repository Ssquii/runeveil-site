SERVER RULES

Freedom with limits.

Runeveil is semi-anarchy, but fairness still matters.

01

No cheating

Do not use hacked clients, combat cheats, x-ray, auto-clickers, or anything that gives an unfair gameplay advantage.

02

No duping or exploiting

Do not abuse bugs, duplication glitches, or broken mechanics to gain items, currency, or progression unfairly.

03

No lag machines

Do not intentionally crash or degrade the server through lag machines, abusive farms, or malicious contraptions.

04

No real-world threats or doxing

Keep conflict in-game. Real-world threats, doxing, harassment campaigns, and serious abuse are not allowed.

05

Respect staff decisions

If a staff member makes a ruling about an exploit or server issue, follow it while the issue is being handled.
