CORE SYSTEMS

The systems behind the server.

Physical Currency

Currency exists as real items that players can earn, hold, trade, store, protect, or lose.

Gear Upgrades

Weapons and armor grow stronger over time, creating a progression path beyond standard survival.

Armor Abilities

Certain armor can gain passive abilities that activate while worn, enabling different builds and strategies.
