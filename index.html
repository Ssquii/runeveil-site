WELCOME TO RUNEVEIL

A server built around danger, progression, and reward.

Runeveil is not a basic survival server. It is built around long-term progression, player conflict, powerful systems, and a dark fantasy identity from top to bottom.

✦

Begin with Events

New players can start earning currency by taking part in server events. These events are one of the fastest ways to begin progressing.

✦

Progression That Matters

Currency and rewards lead into upgrade systems that make your gear stronger, your build better, and your fights more meaningful over time.

✦

Conflict with Real Stakes

In Runeveil, rewards have value. That means PvP, exploration, raiding, and events all connect into the same larger fight for power.

CORE SYSTEMS

The systems that shape the world of Runeveil.

⟡

⚒

Gear Upgrades

Improve your equipment beyond vanilla limits and push your build further over time.

Armor Abilities

WORLD LORE

The Veil did not fall quietly.

Long ago, the Veil shattered the balance of the world. Ancient power bled into the land, twisting it into something darker, richer, and more dangerous.

Fragments of that power remain scattered across Runeveil. Those who rise to claim them can forge strength beyond the limits of ordinary survival.

But power never stays hidden for long. Every event, every battle, and every reward draws stronger enemies from the shadows.

PLAYER JOURNEY

How progression works

  1. Join the world and begin surviving.
  2. Take part in events to start earning currency.
  3. Use progression systems to improve your gear.
  4. Experiment with builds and armor abilities.
  5. Fight for stronger rewards and become a force in Runeveil.

NEW PLAYERS

Where to begin

If you are new, focus on events first. Events help you start building wealth, learning the server, and stepping into the progression systems without needing rare gear immediately.
